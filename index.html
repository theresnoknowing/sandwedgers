Who We Are

Our Purpose

Sandwedgers is a group of people united by a shared love of golf, good company, and the pursuit of glory on the fairways. Each year, we gather at a carefully chosen venue to compete for the most coveted prize in our golfing calendar.

Beyond the competition, the Sandwedgers is about friendship, tradition, and creating memories that last a lifetime. From the first tee shot to the final putt, every moment is cherished — and every champion is immortalised in our Hall of Fame.
